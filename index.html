About Me

My journey into tech started with curiosity—wondering how websites worked, then discovering I could actually build them myself. What began as simple HTML experiments has evolved into a genuine passion for creating software that solves real problems.

What drives me is the intersection of technology and impact. Growing up in Uganda, I've seen firsthand how the right digital solution can transform businesses and communities. That's why I don't just code—I think about the people who'll use what I build.

My philosophy is simple: write code that your future self will thank you for. Clean architecture, thoughtful documentation, and systems that scale. I believe in understanding the "why" before the "how," and in continuous learning as a non-negotiable part of being a developer.
